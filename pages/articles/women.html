Round table dialogue on international womens day

AGED-Network mission involves empowering and promoting women’s sense of self-worth, their ability to determine their own choices, and their right to influence social change for themselves and others.

Women’s empowerment and promoting women’s rights have emerged as a part of a major global movement and is continuing to break new ground in recent years. Days like International Women’s Empowerment Day are also gaining momentum.

AGED-Network ON International Women's day discussed sponsoring women child edducation, educating the population about issues that affect girls and creating opportunities that will provide equal access to opportunities for all genders.
